# Typography

Practical UI ramp, predominantly 4-point. The one exception is **14** — UI body-small is too load-bearing to skip.

**Font:** Arthouse Owned (brand) → Inter → Helvetica → system fallback.

## Semantic roles

| Role | Size | Line | Weight | Use |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Caption | 12 | 16 | Regular | Badges, labels |
| Body Small | 14 | 20 | Regular | Secondary, dense UI |
| Body | 16 | 24 | Regular | Default body |
| Body Large | 20 | 28 | Regular | Lead paragraphs |
| Heading Small | 24 | 32 | Semi Bold | Subsection headings |
| Heading Medium | 32 | 40 | Bold | Section headings |
| Heading Large | 40 | 48 | Bold | Card titles / page sub |
| Display Small | 48 | 56 | Bold | Display |
| Display Medium | 64 | 72 | Black | Hero |
| Display Large | 80 | 88 | Black | Major hero |

Display sizes use tight letter-spacing (-0.02em). Body and small sizes use normal tracking.
